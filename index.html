Suffix Pattern

The base suffixes cycle in groups of 10: K, M, B, T, Qd, Qn, Sx, Sp, Oc, No (representing 103 through 1030). After that, compound suffixes form by combining a base prefix with a group name: De (Decillion, 1033), Vt (Vigintillion, 1063), Tg (Trigintillion, 1093), qg (Quadragintillion, 10123), Qg (Quinquagintillion, 10153), sg (Sexagintillion, 10183), Sg (Septuagintillion, 10213), Og (Octogintillion, 10243), Ng (Nonagintillion, 10273), Ce (Centillion, 10303). Combined suffixes stack: e.g. SpDe = Sp x De = 1024 x 1033 = 1057.
